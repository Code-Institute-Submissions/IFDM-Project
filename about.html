The Group Brands:


A.S. Création markets its wallpaper products under the brands "innova", "A.S. Création", "livingwalls" and "Architects Paper".

In addition, there are attractive licensing brands in the wallpaper offer that enrich the entire product range, that’s why we are sure that you will find with us whatever you wish and like to find your choice to decorate or renovate your walls.

In the "Fashion and lifestyle brands" segment, they are developing wallpapers with VERSACE wallpaper, ESPRIT home, Jette Joop, Daniel Hechter and Versace.

In the "Furnishing and women's magazines" branch, there is a wallpaper cooperation with Schöner Wohnen and Brigitte Home.

MICHALSKY LIVING, Raffi from the Dutch designer Maarten Vrolijk and the German designer Lars Contzen has already developed many wallpaper collections for A.S. Création.

Under the "Architects Paper", brand A.S. Création markets, for example, wallpapers from the star architect Hadi Teherani, Ingo Maurer and from the pen of Porsche Design Studios.

Architects Paper, is the international operational premium and object brand of Europe’s largest wallpaper manufacturer, A.S. Creation Tapeten AG.

Architects Paper stands for exclusive, individual wall solutions such as handmade single runs for architects, designers, interior fitters, developers and property planners.

In this process, The innovative wallpapers, digital prints and wall panels (all CE approved) for example are used in hotels, company HQs, high-value gastronomy in shops or in care areas. The vast majority of the collection is finished by means of costly and elaborate special processes at the company and production locations in Oberberg’s Gummersbach.

Besides the wallpapers, we deal in high-quality decorative fabrics from Indes Fuggerhaus Textil GmbH. a wholly-owned subsidiary company by A.S.
